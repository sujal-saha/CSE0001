## The Social Media Checklist

| Category | Do's (Best Practices) | Don'ts (Avoid These) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Privacy** | **Do** audit your privacy settings regularly to ensure only friends see personal posts. | **Don't** post your real-time location or sensitive data like your student ID or home address. |
| **Professionalism** | **Do** use platforms like LinkedIn to highlight your internships, projects, and skills. | **Don't** engage in "venting" about professors, classmates, or employers online. |
| **Critical Thinking** | **Do** verify information from multiple sources before sharing news or "viral" claims. | **Don't** share content that is inflammatory, discriminatory, or promotes misinformation. |
| **Engagement** | **Do** interact with accounts related to your field of study to network and learn. | **Don't** participate in "internet dogpiling" or online harassment. |
| **Digital Wellness** | **Do** set screen time limits to ensure social media doesn't interfere with sleep or studies. | **Don't** tie your self-worth to engagement metrics like likes, shares, or follower counts. |
